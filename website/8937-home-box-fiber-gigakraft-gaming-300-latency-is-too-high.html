Hi Roman,

I finally reached English speaking customer service representative. He by my request contacted layer 2 support engineer.

Docsis 3.1 is confirmed for my link. Unfortunately it's Docsis 3.1 pure setup without a latency optimization. Because of that an average 10ms round trip latency is what can be achieved. To get a lower latency, Low Latency Docsis 3.1 (LLD)  should be implemented.
