Hi @ Dap

You can do a one-time top-up with a credit card on this page:

https://ladezone.magenta.at/pay/recharge.jsp

You only need to top-up the minimum amount of 10€ in order to extend the lifespan of your prepaid SIM by another year.

Online Banking is not supported with Italian banks.

If this method does not work for some reason, you can also buy a top-up voucher online.

Here are some options:

  • https://www.interspar.at/shop/haushalt/magenta-handyladeguthaben-10-/p/7232893 (credit card, PayPal)
  • https://www.ladebon.at/product/magenta (credit card)
  • https://guthaben.at/kategorie/magenta/ (credit card, PayPal)

I hope this information can be of help to you.

regards NTM
