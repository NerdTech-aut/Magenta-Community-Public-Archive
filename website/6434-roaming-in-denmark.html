Is it a problem with roaming in general (so you don't get any connection) or just data roaming?

If you can't connect at all have you tried a manual network search?

In Norway, a Magenta plan should work on Telenor and Telia.

If your problem is only with data roaming, have you enabled data roaming in the settings app?

It could also be the case that data roaming is locked for your sim and you might need to unlock it through your "Mein Magenta".
