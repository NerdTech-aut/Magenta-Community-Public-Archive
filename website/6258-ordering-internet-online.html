When ordering online you can enter an alternative delivery address for the modem.

The billing starts when you start using your internet connection but at the latest five days after the connection was activated.

The connection should be activated within 1 - 2 weeks.

Also keep in mind that a technican might need to activate your connection and maybe needs to check the connection from inside the flat.

It woulnd't hurt if you check with Magentas' customer service to see what would need to be done.

Magenta usually delivers with DPD. If you are not home the package would typically be brought to a partner store for pickup.

If the company you are with allows it, it might be better sent it there as some of the DPD partner stores might be closed due to the 2nd lockdown.

It is hard to say when it would be best to order in the current situation.

If there are delays, you might not have it when you move in. If a technician needs access to the flat, you will need to get an appointment when you already moved in (which also might not be on December 1st).

So yea probably order now. If everything goes smoothly you have it by December 1st but (and if it’s too fast might lose a day or two).

But in the case, it doesn't do smoothly you can always get a prepaid Sim with unlimited data.

Magenta would also offer an unlimited data prepaid option but as you need a special stater pack for this plan you can get a prepaid sim with an unlimited data option at a lot of supermarkets, gas stations, etc.
